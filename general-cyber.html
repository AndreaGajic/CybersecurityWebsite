General Cybersecurity Fun Stuff

In this page, you will be learning about the most secure practices to interact with the world around you online

Check the Fun Stuff Page to try the quiz to test your skills!

Personal Information is any information that can be used to identify you:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Where you go to school
  • Where your parents or guardians work

    • How do I keep my Personal Information safe?

  • Only share it with people you trust
  • Never post anything you want to keep private
  • Do not over-share
  • Do not post your location

    • Phising is an email scam in which someone tries to gather personal or financial information from you. Phishing emails are typically:

  • Are from people you do not know
  • Ask for Personal Information
  • Have suspicious attachments

    • Malware is any computer program that is designed to do things that are harmful or unwanted by a computer’s legitimate user…YOU! Types of malware include:

  • Viruses
  • Worms
  • Trojan Horses
  • Many more…

    • Protect yourself from malware by using an antivirus, or an anti malware software which will...

  • Scan for malware
  • Alert if malware is found
  • Remove malware

    • Firewalls

    Firewalls are software or hardware that block viruses, worms, Trojan horses, and other types of malware from getting into a computer
    Firewalls can also block data from leaving a computer, which is useful if a virus is trying to steal information

    Passwords

  • Passwords are used to lock a computer or user account and protect things in it
  • Passwords are a type of Personal Information
  • Never share your Password
  • Create strong Passwords to keep your information secure
  • Passwords should be hard for others to guess, but easy for your to remember

    • Strong Passwords are difficult to guess. They usually have at least:

  • Letters
  • Numbers
  • Symbols
  • At least 8 characters

    • The guest account is a way to get into your computer without a username or password so make sure to disable your guest account to be safe.
